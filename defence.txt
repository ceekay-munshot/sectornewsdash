<summary># Executive Summary
As of 2026-04-28, India defence newsflow over the last 60 days remains decisively positive, led by strong order inflows at BEL, execution milestones across naval shipyards, a meaningful overseas expansion by Mazagon Dock, and emerging procurement opportunities in drones, radars, aerial munitions, and indigenous testing infrastructure. Near-term investable signals are strongest in listed defence electronics and shipbuilding names, while broader ecosystem demand remains supported by indigenisation and export scaling.

## Key Findings
- 2026-04-28: Sigma Advanced Systems secured a ₹3,800 crore, 7-year Rolls-Royce aerospace parts contract, indicating continued global supply-chain integration for Indian aerospace manufacturing.
- 2026-04-28: DRDO and the Indian Navy achieved a hydrodynamic testing milestone for a frontline warship project, reducing programme risk for downstream naval builds.
- 2026-04-28: The IAF initiated a requirement for an indigenous drone-swarm detection radar, pointing to a new domestic counter-UAS sensing opportunity.
- 2026-04-28: Adani Defence delivered the first batch of 2,000 Prahar light machine guns to the Army, marking transition from order to execution.
- 2026-04-24: Mazagon Dock signed a corrosion-technology MoU with NACE India, relevant for lifecycle cost, quality, and execution performance in shipbuilding.
- 2026-04-23: BEL received fresh orders worth ₹569 crore, sustaining order momentum after a strong FY26 close.
- 2026-04-21: BEL entered an MoU with BMIT to strengthen indigenous design and production in advanced defence technologies.
- 2026-04-20: The fourth Diving Support Craft under an Indian Navy project was launched, reflecting continued execution in naval auxiliary platforms.
- 2026-04-15: Telangana’s aerospace and defence exports exceeded ₹32,000 crore in FY26, reinforcing localisation and cluster-scale manufacturing trends.
- 2026-04-13: DRDO unveiled amphibious battle platforms with crewless turrets and anti-tank missile capability, signalling future indigenous armoured procurement potential.
- 2026-04-13: A Large Cavitation Tunnel was approved at NSTL, Visakhapatnam, expanding domestic naval testing infrastructure.
- 2026-04-13: The IAF tied up with GE for a Tejas engine repair and overhaul facility, a fleet-availability positive for the Tejas ecosystem.
- 2026-04-09: Mazagon Dock completed acquisition of a 51% stake in Colombo Dockyard, adding overseas repair and shipbuilding capacity.
- 2026-04-06: The Army published an unmanned systems roadmap prioritising low-cost drones and domestic innovation, supportive for Indian drone vendors.

- 2026-04-01: BEL reported record FY26 turnover of ₹26,750 crore, demonstrating strong execution at scale.

- 2026-04-01: BEL announced a USD 33 million export order for IoT devices, supporting export diversification and hard-currency revenue.
