## Actionable Recommendations
- **Prioritize** PV OEMs and core ancillaries with high exposure to sustained dispatch recovery, especially where higher utilization can improve operating leverage.
- **Monitor** FADA retail trends versus SIAM wholesales to detect inventory build-up, especially in PV and 2W channels.
- **Favor** suppliers aligned to EV, hybrid and CNG platforms, as clean-mobility mix is rising faster than overall market growth.
- **Track** Tata Motors’ EV lead against likely competitive responses from M&M, Hyundai, MG and Maruti, as pricing and launch intensity may increase in FY27.
- **Be selective** on EV volume assumptions in the mass market; current data suggests premium and mid-market segments are scaling faster than entry-level adoption.
- **Watch** policy and interest-rate sensitivity closely, since SIAM explicitly linked demand support to tax and financing relief.
- **Use** VAHAN and FADA together as independent checks on demand quality before extrapolating wholesale-led growth into earnings.
